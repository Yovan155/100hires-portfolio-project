## Research Goal

This project researches how leading SEO and growth marketing experts use AI to improve content creation, content scaling, topical authority, search visibility, and organic growth for B2B SaaS companies.

The collected materials include LinkedIn posts, YouTube transcripts, interviews, and educational content from practitioners actively working in SEO and growth marketing.
