## Key Research Convergences

All ten experts have converged, independently, on two underlying claims by mid-2026:

1. **Ranking is necessary but no longer sufficient.** Citation, mention, and brand-recall inside AI answer surfaces (AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) are now treated as a distinct, measurable layer sitting on top of classic organic SEO.

2. **Third-party corroboration is weighted heavily by AI retrieval systems.** Editorial mentions, Reddit, forums, podcasts, and LinkedIn are weighted more heavily than most B2B SaaS content teams currently account for in their operating model.
