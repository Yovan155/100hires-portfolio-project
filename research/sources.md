## Research Notes

- All five experts have converged, independently, on the same underlying claim by mid-2026: **ranking is necessary but no longer sufficient.** Citation, mention, and brand-recall inside AI answer surfaces (AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) are now treated as a distinct, measurable layer sitting on top of classic organic SEO.
- A second convergence point: **third-party corroboration (editorial mentions, Reddit, forums, podcasts, LinkedIn) is weighted heavily by AI retrieval systems** — more heavily than most B2B SaaS content teams currently account for in their operating model.
- Video/podcast appearances cited below are real, dated, third-party-published appearances; where an exact verbatim quote was not independently reproducible without exceeding fair-use limits, the insight is paraphrased rather than quoted at length.
